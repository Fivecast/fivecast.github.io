D2D CRC wins the Defence Teaming Centre ‘Growing & Emerging’ Award for Fivecast.

The Data to Decisions Cooperative Research Centre has received the ‘Growing & Emerging’ Award from the Defence Teaming Centre as part of the 2019 Annual DTC Defence Industry Dinner and Awards Ceremony. The award was received in recognition of the formation and growth of Fivecast. Brenton and Duane from the Fivecast team were on hand to receive the award in front of 665 people at the Adelaide Convention Centre.
