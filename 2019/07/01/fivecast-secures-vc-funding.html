Fivecast Secures VC Funding And Announces Its Expansion Into The US Market

Fivecast has secured a $4 million investment round from Main Sequence Ventures’ CSIRO Innovation Fund and the South Australian Venture Capital Fund (SAVCF). This round will drive international expansion and product growth for the company’s key product line, Fivecast Insight.

An official press release is available for download here and further discussions about the investment and the companies expansion plans are available in a recent Australian Financial Review article.
