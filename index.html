About Fivecast

Fivecast delivers insights from data.

Fivecast was formed through a collaboration between Australia's national security agencies and various research organisations. Leading organisations turn to Fivecast when they need risk-based analytics to answer investigative and intelligence challenges.

Insight and Discovery  are powerful open-source data collection and analysis platforms that combine to deliver insights from vast quantities of unstructured, multimedia data. Purpose-built to address the highest priority use cases in the national security, law enforcement and defence markets, Fivecast has been proven to deliver real value through increased productivity and actionable insights.

Solutions for entity, network and thematic analysis

Fivecast Insight – Available now

Entity

Fivecast Insight provides analysts with a detailed understanding of an online entity to answer key questions relevant to their mission. The sophisticated detectors and analytical tools provided with Fivecast Insight have the ability for deep-detection of factors of interest, visualisations of content and measurement of changes over time.

Network

Fivecast Insight provides the ability to understand the collective content and behaviour of a group of related or similar entities by placing them into a single investigation. Analysts can then examine the relationships between entities, and uncover previously unknown connections between key actors. It allows analysts to focus on entities with the highest engagement with content of interest.

Fivecast Discovery – Coming in 2018

DISCOVERY

Fivecast Discovery provides the ability for user-driven collection and thematic analysis of population-level open source information. Advanced geo-tagging and deep-learning techniques provide analysts with early indicators and warnings to understand engagement with specific narratives, detect the emergence of new topics and even the ability to predict future events.

Key Features

  • Sophisticated Analytics

    Fivecast software employs a range of sophisticated configurable detectors that work across text and image content that can be configured for key terms or phrases, objects and scenes in images, and quoted material.

  • Visualisation & Exploration of Data

    Users can visualise changes to the social network, content and detections over time. All of the content is indexed so analysts can quickly search across large volumes of stored historical data.

  • Automated Change Detection & Alerts

    The tools can be set to continuously ingest new content as it is produced and proactively alert analysts of changes. Automation of monitoring process saves countless hours.

  • Foresight

    Fivecast provides prediction not detection. A range of predictive geo-political risk analytics models give deep insight and allow agencies to mitigate risks rather than react to potentially disruptive events.

  • Targeted & Automated Collection & Storage

    Fivecast has automated the collection of data from a range of open-source platforms.

  • Organised User-Configurable Platform

    The easy to use and intuitive user interface allows automation of repetitive tasks, focusing analysts on critical insights.
