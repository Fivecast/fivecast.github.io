Software Engineer (Senior)

Adelaide, Australia

About

Fivecast is hiring a Senior Software Engineer to join our best-practice development team.

We write modern webapps using React.JS and back-end services in Java, with a healthy dose of automation and tooling to help maintain quality while moving fast. We do agile the right way, regularly shipping to production, listening to users and responding to feedback.

You’ll be working with experienced people on challenging software problems, applying complex machine learning models developed by our Data Science team to large volumes of unstructured data. Our team is cross-functional, contributing to testing, operations and support as required. We’re mainly looking for strong Javascript/Java developers, but a broader skillset will be highly valued.

The position is 12 months fixed-term with permanent entitlements and strong opportunities for extension.

Requirements

Minimum NV1 Defence clearance or eligibility to acquire one is mandatory (requires Australian Citizenship).

Must Have

  • Degree level qualifications e.g. Computer Science, Software Engineering
  • Minimum 5 years of experience in Software Engineering roles
  • Experience designing, implementing and testing software applications in Java
  • Experience with web front-end technologies e.g. HTML, CSS, and Javascript frameworks e.g. React.JS, Angular
  • Experience using relational databases and designing relational data models
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to work within an agile team

Desirable

  • Experience with search indexes or NoSQL document stores e.g. Elasticsearch, Solr, MongoDB
  • An understanding of data science and machine learning techniques
